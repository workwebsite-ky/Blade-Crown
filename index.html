Our Story

Who We Are

A Legacy of Craft

Blade & Crown was born in 2015 with a single conviction: that every gentleman deserves a grooming experience worthy of his ambitions. Founded by master barber William Cross, our shop was designed as a sanctuary — a place where the ancient craft of barbering meets modern luxury.

What began as a two-chair shop in the heart of the city has grown into one of the most respected grooming establishments in the region. Our team of 24 master barbers has collectively trained across London, New York, and Milan — bringing global techniques to every appointment.

Today, Blade & Crown holds the trust of executives, athletes, artists, and gentlemen of all walks of life. The name on the door is a promise: when you sit in our chair, you leave as royalty.
